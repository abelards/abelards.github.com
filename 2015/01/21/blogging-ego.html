The Blogging Ego

In here as in life, I hope I don’t have much ego showing. Being proud and confident is nice but ego has a tendency to show up and ruin good things.

When blogs were hip I was being even more hipster by despising them. Sorry, teenage years. On the other hand, nobody had to suffer the bad content I would have written then.

Now I have created enough content I want to share, mostly - talks I made at events and conferences - translations of content I thought should be shared in french - questions I’ve been asked enough times to structure

so it’s not really a blogging ego, rather a way to get back control of my content and “being the change I want to bring the world”.

So for now the biggest effort I’m putting in there is just reducing posts to bite-sized chunks. I have an horrible tendency to make things huge and ugly, so I’ll leave it at that.

Cheers,
