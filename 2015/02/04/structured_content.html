On Structured Content

I’m forcing myself to small-sized chunks of content.

There’s also two experiments I’d like to make, linking bits of my opinions to the others:

  1. make their relationships obvious
  2. not mixing ideas and having them as atomic as possible
  3. finding myself which bits of my philosophy most shape my ideas
  4. finding which bits of my philosophy are most useful / explain most things
  5. running a PageRank or reverse-PageRank algo on it to find the “roots” of it

I suppose this requires a discipline and a lot of rework I probably won’t be able / courageous enough to make. But it’d be nice to try, and if someone else does it I’d find it interesting and motivating :)
