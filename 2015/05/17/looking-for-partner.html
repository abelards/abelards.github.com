Looking for dev partner

As a developer and as ParisRB’s organiser, I often get asked if I want to be a partner in a new startup adventure.

First, I’m already a partner and already in an adventure.

You probably already know:

  • it’s hard to find good devs,
  • the devs you find are available for a reason
  • you probably can’t judge a developer’s skill

So you’ve put up a job offer which:

  • is the same as every other we get each week
  • uses words which do not convey equality
  • or words easily mistaken for abusive
  • might just be looking for a Gullible Young

And then you ask me for referrals, but:

  • I probably don’t have enough info to bother them
  • repeatedly bothering one’s ‘network’ loses it
  • top-talent referral is worth a lot

I do wish you the best of luck and hope we all grow the community. Companies with hiring problems will turn to other technologies which is not in our best interest either.

I’m not doing this to repel you or to sound like a passive-agressive smartass. I will be glad to help if only… you help me helping you.
