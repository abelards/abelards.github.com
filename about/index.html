About

Hi, my name is Sylvain Abélard.

I have opinons and I share them. This whole site is CC-BY-SA and you can contribute on GitHub.

You will find my public activity: talks, events, and blog. You will also find translations of texts I liked and I cared to translate, and a few questions I’ve been asked enough / topics I cared enough about to grow a structured answer / opinion about it.

Feel free to share them, don’t fear for IP stuff (nor, I hope, for blogging ego). I simply created this website to get back the control of content I create.
