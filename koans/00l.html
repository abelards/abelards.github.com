Give them enlightenment

A certain monk’s users came to hom menacingly. He sought counsel to master Kaimu.

“Give them a spreadsheet”, he said. The monk rushed off in a hurry, fearing the customers were already sharpening tools to behead him.

Kaimu sipped his tea. “When a student does not wish to hear the second part of a lesson, the Master must not feel hurt in his pride: the World will teach the young a much harsher lesson.”
