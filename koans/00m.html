A life of their own

The same monk came back, long after, to master Kaimu.

“Did you give them a spreadsheet?” the master asked.

“I did, but they wanted change, and they found it broke several processes, and now all of them want my head saying I was responsible!”

“How did this happen, did they not ask you to do it?”

“I did, but then they wanted this and that.”

“They wanted me to add whatever.”

“They forgot the spec.”

“They complained about performance.”

“They forgot about my beautiful application.”

“They gave more contracts to people who dirtied everything.”

“And did you display the sheets like I told you?” “I did, but now it’s all dirty and some pieces were torn off.”

“The World is a harsh master.”
