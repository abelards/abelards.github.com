One Shoe

The Temple is composed of many clans, and keeping rivalries at bay is not an easy thing. Luckily, the Masters devised long ago an appropriate punishment.

One certain monk was caught mocking the One Shoe Clan. “Nothing they do is elegant, everything is disposable. Get rid of them already, and that abomination PHP they develop in their tower!”

He was summoned by master Kaimu.

Walking a path of burning coals, barefoot, was his punishment.
